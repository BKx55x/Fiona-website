About Fiona

HI! I'M FIONA KARWOSKI I am currently in my third year as an interior design student with a minor in architecture. I attend SCAD! The Savannah College of Art & Design in Savannah, Georgia, where the city's history and vibrant culture continuously inspire my work. Interior Design has been a longterm passion of mine however interior design is not where my love for art begins or ends. I have a passion for all things design, from crocheting and knitting to graphic design I love to collect new hobbies and develop my creative skills. Loving all things vintage, I have a deep appreciation for vintage furniture and decor and love breathing a new life into second hand pieces.
