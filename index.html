Ornamented Minimalism Concept

Tea is important to Turkish culture, and this design of a tea room showcases a modern rendition of the authentic and inviting space that reflects the rich cultural heritage of Turkey. The space fosters socialization and reflects warmth and hospitality. The tranquil yet vibrant color palette, in addition to traditional Turkish motifs, celebrates the interaction surrounding tea and Turkish traditions. The space offers visitors a modernized and immersive cultural experience.

About Fiona

I am Fiona Karwoski! I attend the Savannah College of Art & Design in Savannah, Georgia, where the city's history and vibrant culture continuously inspire my work. I am in my third year as an interior design student with a minor in architecture. Through my diverse creative interests, I have cultivated a well-rounded design philosophy that values innovation, creativity, and functionality. I believe that every space has the potential to tell a story, and my goal as an interior designer is to create environments that evoke emotion and foster connection. As I continue my studies and refine my skills, I am excited to explore new ways of blending art and design to push boundaries and make a meaningful impact on the spaces people inhabit.
